This map was created using log entries and correspondence found in U.S. Office of Naval Records and Library, Naval Documents Related to the United States Wars with the Barbary Powers, 6 vols. (Washington: U.S. Govt. Print. Off., 1939).

To determine approximate latitudes and longitudes based on the information in the logs, sometimes calculations are necessary. To perform these calculations, I used this series of scripts by Chris Veness.
